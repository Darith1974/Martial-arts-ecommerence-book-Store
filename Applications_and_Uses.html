Sport

One of the most obvious uses of martial arts is as regards Sport. Alot of martial art systems are great ways of keeping one fit both physically and mentally. It provides training for alot of muscles in the body. It also teaches self defence as well. Although one must take a word of caution here. If one wants to luarn self defence properly then one must choose the system properly. Some systems are deliberatley more sport oriented and are not that effective on the street. That been said some teachers and hence their systems are deliberatley more sport oreinted in order to get people more interested with the reason been that some training is better than none!

Self Defence

Martial Art systems were primarily develop with self defence and hence combat in mind. Over thousands of years individulas, familes, clans, nations(Plus their Military/Police forces) develop means to defend one selves and hence attack if neccessary. Primarily martail arts a take form of using legs, arms and any joint as a means of strike plus also disabling their opponents using counter strikes or applying even pressure to nerve points.

Personnel development

Martial Arts can be used to teach discipline, restraint, manners, and even aggression when neccessary. Boxing is such a prime example which has been used in western society to attain such values.
